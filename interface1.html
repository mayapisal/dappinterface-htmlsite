Crisis Relief DApp Router

A centralized platform for managing disaster response logistics


Visualize and track available resources like food, water, medical supplies, and personnel across different regions. Mapping ensures better allocation and prevents resource clustering or scarcity.


Monitor demand signals from affected areas in real-time. Prioritize needs based on severity, urgency, and population size to coordinate a timely and proportional response.


Dynamically assign available resources to demand points. This tool uses priority-based or AI-enhanced logic to optimize delivery routes and match needs with the nearest resource hubs.


Geolocated zones where requests for assistance originate. Each demand point includes critical data like type of need, urgency level, and contact details for local coordinators.
